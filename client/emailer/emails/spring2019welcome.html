Welcome Huskies

It's time for another season of flag football. My name is Brett van Beynum and I'll be the coaching the Huskies this season. I'm looking forward to another season of football and can't wait to meeting everyone at our first practice. We'll have a number of returning players from out last season and I'm looking forward to welcoming some new members to the team. For those new to the team, we are a team that loves have fun and has lots of spirit (and a cheering section). For those who've been playing flag football before, much of this will be familiar. If anyone has any questions please feel free to reach out to me anytime.

This season we will be the University of Washington Huskies again. The rec department will be providing us with shirts, socks and flags. Our team colors will be white shirts (sorry moms) with purple lettering, black shorts and purple socks. The shirts and socks usually arrive the week before the first game.

Games

Games are scheduled set to start on Saturday March 16th. Games will be primarily on Saturdays, but there may be a few Friday games also. Once the games start, we will no longer have the Saturday practice. Everyone is important at the games and if you know before that you won't be able to make a game, please let me know as soon as possible so we can plan accordingly.

The game schedule should be sent out the week of 2/25. I will send out the schedule to everyone once it is available. About 5 days after each game, the rec department will usually update the results on the website.
