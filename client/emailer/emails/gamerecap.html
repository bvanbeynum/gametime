Game Recap

Wow! What a great game. Everyone really played a part in this win. Everyone was catching the ball, and almost everyone had a touchdown. This result shows how strong we are as a team, and how we can all pull together for a critical win.

This is the win that secures the Huskies as the SoCon conference champions, and a place in the championship bowl game. It has been a lot of hard work and extra practice, with learning new plays, lots of flag pulling, and new drills all season. Everyone on the team should feel proud of what they have personally done as part of this team.

Next week we will play the Gamecocks. This is a team that will rely heavily on their run game, but that is no different than the last 3 teams we've faced. With how strong our defense has been playing, and our ability to contain and pull flags, I think this will be another great game for us. If we can force them to the air, we'll be able to close out the season with a perfect record.


Go Huskies!!!
-Brett van Beynum
