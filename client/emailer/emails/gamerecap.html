Game Recap

We are continuing the steamroller that is the Huskies. Everyone had a great game, and was able to get into a position to make a play. We made a big improvement this week on our route running. The improvement in route running is going to be the key as we get later in the season and all other teams continue to get better also. We are going to start seeing that all the other teams are going to start to gel and the games will become more difficult. The way we are able to continue to get the ball to everyone highlights everyone's ability to find their open space.

As with last week, our defense was our key to the game. We see them starting to try new things with initial success, but each time our defense is able to adjust to any tactic. The ability of our defense to be so dynamic highlights what a talented team we have. I would especially like to call out how even when we miss a flag, we are slowing down the runner and allowing the rest of the team to drop in to support. As long as we can slow down the runner, we can keep the yardage down for only short gains.

It is interesting to see that no matter how the kids are at practice, they always turn it on for the game. I'm happy to see that all the kids come out to the games ready to give it their all. It is great to see that they have so much fun with each other, and then to see them support each other as much as they do during the games. This season is going to be a fun season.
Thanks everyone for a fun game.


Go Huskies!!!
-Brett van Beynum
