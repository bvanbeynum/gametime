Game Recap

This was such a good game. We knew this was going to be the toughest game of the season (it was the championship after all). From the first snap, I could see that this was going to be a good game. The kids played their heart out, and they should be incredibly proud of how well they did.

On defense they did such a good job of stopping their offense. They were able to keep Sawyer contained, which is something that no other team had been able to do. Coach Tim had a new plan for the defense and the team did such a good job executing. The defense was able to force them to throw where we were able to cover well. They were able to take advantage of small yardage to move the ball enough to score the three times. But it was overall an excellent effort to contain them.

The offense did a good job moving the ball against a tough defense. We were able to take advantage of the short yardage also. This allowed us to move the ball consistently, but we just had a few times that we didn't punch the ball in when we were at the goal line. The team did a great job getting the ball there, but we just needed to close one more of the drives. It was really a great job of moving the ball and it was a great team effort. This defense was very good at staying in their zones and not following the fakes we were trying to get them to bite on. This made it difficult to get them wide open that we've been able to do with other teams.

I want to thank everyone, kids, parents and definitely everyone who helped coach, for a great season! We could not have done it, and had such a good time without everyone.


Go Huskies!!!
-Brett van Beynum
