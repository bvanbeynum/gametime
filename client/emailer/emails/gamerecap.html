Game Recap

What a great game. Everyone should be very proud of how they played today. As I was telling them after the game how important everyone in this win because this is a team effort. Everyone is important on the team, if they are the primary target, they are clearing out a defender, or if they are protecting a zone on defense. I was very happy how well everyone played for their first game. The first games are always the most difficult because of how new everything is. This is especially true for those new to the 10U division with all the different rules and the speed of the game. They have worked very hard to get to this point today and I think it really shows in the score.

I would like to send a special shout out to our team cheer squad. It is great to have such great support from the families. It really makes it fun when everyone is enjoying watching the games and is sharing their support. These games are such fun and it really gets the kids excited when they can hear the support from their biggest fans. Especially when we have these really hot games, it can be difficult for the kids to stay engaged, but hearing the support from the families is so helpful. Thank you all for your support and for making the games fun for everyone.
