Game Recap

Another great game, and another great win. This week we played one of the toughest teams of the division, and the Huskies came to win. We had a slow start getting points on the board, but after Turner's pick 6 (from across the field), the steam roller started rolling. We have everyone get a shot at the ball and we had some amazing plays on the field that shows that we have one of the best teams in the division.

As great as the offense was, it was our defense that won the game this week. The team came out strong and made sure not to give up any points early in the game. Even after the Boilermakers got great field position after the interception, they were still not able to walk away with any points. After the half, they made a change in quarterback and restarted their offense. They were able to spread us out a bit, but by the end of the game, we stood strong and the defense saved the day by keeping them out of the end zone for the tie/win.

For those that were bored with our first game, I hope this game was a little more exciting for everyone. It was fun to see how we can even get the other teams barking, but it sounds like our teams still barks LOUDEST!!! It was a fun game for me, and this is a fun team and season. I hope everyone enjoys the win after all the hard work they have put in these past 2 weeks. Also, I'd like to say a big thanks to Turner's mom, Carissa, for the amazing goal line video.
