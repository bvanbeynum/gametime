Game Recap

Tough game this week. After the slow start, the defense really shut the Bulldogs down. After getting the 2 picks, they decided that the only option was to keep it on the ground. This really helped us to burn quite a bit of time off the clock. Now we just need to shorten the distance they are getting on each run. I hope all the kids are ready for some flag pulling practice this week.

On offense we will need to work on throwing and catching. We had 9 incomplete passes today which could really help us to convert in those 3rd down situations. It looks like more and more teams are trying to take Turner out of the play, but since we play as a team this won't stop us. Today we heard the Bulldogs coach say, "just cover Turner" and that allowed Cam to be wide open for the touchdown. This just goes to show how everyone is important on the team, no matter what route they are running.

Next week we will have Notre Dame, and they are looking for their first win. This means they will be working extra hard this week to put a stop to us. I hope everyone is ready for another fun and exciting all-out game next week.

I really want to thank all the coaches for helping out each week. I could not do everything without the help of Tim, Craig, Sean and Cary. Each week they keep the team organized and are a large part of why this team is such a fun team and why we keep getting better each week.

Thanks everyone for a fun game.


Go Huskies!!!
