Welcome Huskies

Welcome to 2018 fall flag football season! My name is Brett van Beynum and I'll be the coach for this season. I'm excited to start another season of flag football and look forward to meeting everyone at our first practice. This is my second season coaching 10U flag football, and it is great to see some returning players, and also to welcome some new players. For those who've been playing flag footbal for a while, much of this will be familiar, but if anyone has any questions please feel free to reach out to me anytime.

This season our team will be the University of Washington Huskies. The rec department will be providing us with shirts, socks and flags. Our team colors this season will be white shirts (sorry moms) with purple lettering, black shorts and purple socks. The shirts and socks usually arrive the week before the first game.

Games

Games are tentatively set to start on Saturday September 8th. Games will be primarily on Saturdays, but there may be a few Friday games also. Once the games start, we will no longer have the Saturday practice. Everyone is important at the games and if you know before that you won't be able to make a game, please let me know as soon as possible so we can plan accordingly.

The game schedule will be sent out about 1 week before the games are scheduled to begin. I will send out the schedule to everyone once it is available. About 5 days after each game, the rec department will usually update the results on the website.
