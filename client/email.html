Welcome Seahawks

Welcome to 2019 fall flag football season! My name is Brett van Beynum and I'll be the coach for the Seahawks. I'm excited to start another season of flag football and look forward to meeting everyone at our first practice. If anyone has any questions please feel free to reach out to me anytime.
